What is Sweet Memories

Sweet Memories is an event planner. Our crew will turn the clients event fantasy into a reality. Sweet Memories will enhance their dream in order to make our clients happy, satisfied and become one of their best memories to be remembered.

Mission, Vision and Objective

Mission

To help realize the dreams desired by the customers in the success of  various events with the services provided by Sweet Memories. Sweet Memories will also help customers create the most beautiful and unforgettable memories

Vision

·  To be one of the renowned event  planners companies and to be the first choice by customers in event planning.

Objective

. To bring happiness to everyone whenever they receives gift 

. To meet the demands and satisfaction of customers.

 . To be part of remarkable memories created by customers
